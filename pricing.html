  • Which Is The Best Plan For Me?

    A short answer to the question above is, it depends on your business needs:

    Starter: If most of your business is operated offline and wants to have a digital presence the Starter Plan is going to be the best suit for your job.

    Standard: If you want things such as a small blog and newsletter functionalities on your site then the Standard Plan is the way to go.

    Premium: If you want to have an eCommerce store and stuff such as PWA or things that require robust backend our Premium Plan is going to match all your needs.
